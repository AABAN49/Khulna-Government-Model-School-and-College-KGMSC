The Computer Club of Khulna Government Model School and College (KGMSC) is a dedicated co-curricular program aimed at promoting digital literacy, programming skills, problem-solving abilities, and technological awareness among students. At khulna government model school and college, the Computer Club provides a platform for students to explore information technology, develop coding skills, engage in creative projects, and enhance their digital proficiency. The club also encourages teamwork, innovation, and practical learning.
