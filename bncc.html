Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC):

BNCC at KGMSC:

The Bangladesh National Cadet Corps (BNCC) is an important and prestigious co-curricular program at Khulna Government Model School and College (KGMSC). The BNCC program plays a vital role in developing discipline, leadership, patriotism, and physical fitness among students.

At khulna government model school and college, BNCC training is designed to prepare students to become responsible citizens who are mentally strong, physically fit, and morally disciplined. The BNCC unit of KGMSC follows the official guidelines of Bangladesh National Cadet Corps and operates under proper supervision.

Importance of BNCC in Student Life:

BNCC is more than just a training program; it is a platform that shapes students into confident leaders. Students who participate in BNCC at KGMSC learn valuable life skills that help them in both academic and personal life.

BNCC helps students to:

1.Develop discipline and punctuality
2.Improve leadership qualities
3.Build physical fitness and endurance
4.Learn teamwork and cooperation
5.Gain confidence and responsibility

At khulna government model school and college, BNCC is considered a powerful tool for character building.

The main objectives of the BNCC program at KGMSC are aligned with national goals and educational values.

Our BNCC objectives include:

Khulna government model school and college aims to create disciplined and dedicated cadets through BNCC training.
